About

Hi, I’m Erin! I’m an early-career UX & UI designer that specializes in web design and accessibility.

My goal as a designer is to make the internet more beautiful and accessible. I transitioned to design from the dance and theater world in 2022 and from that I take a passion for inclusion and community. I want to make sure your website can be used by everyone, regardless of how they access it.

In addition to design, I've taken the time to gain skills in front-end development so I can effectively communicate with other members of my team. I strive to never stop learning, growing, and changing both as a designer and as a person.

Let’s work together to breathe new life into your online presence.
