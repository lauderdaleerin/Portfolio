About

Hi! I’m Erin! I’m an artist, designer, and developer.

My goal as a creative is to elevate the user experience through art and design to be more accessible. Technology should be designed for the end-user, and I help facilitate that.

I love bold graphics and bright colors, but if you don’t, that’s okay! Design may be subjective, but accessibility is not. I’m passionate about improving your website design and user-friendliness for all.

Let’s work together to breathe new life into your online presence.
