UX Design

As a UX designer, I focus on creating user-centered designs that are both intuitive and enjoyable. I begin my design process by conducting research to understand your user’s needs and business goals, then imagine prospective users and scenarios to guide the design process. I will create wireframes and prototypes to explore ideas and gather feedback, and conduct usability tests to refine the user experience. I will then design interactive elements and visual aesthetics to ensure your web design is both functional and appealing. Throughout the process, will collaborate with you, the developers, and any other stakeholders to align the design with the product vision and continuously improve based on user feedback. My ultimate aim is to deliver a product that meets your business objectives while providing a seamless, satisfying experience for your users.

On previous UX projects, I have worked with Figma, Adobe XD, and InVision as well as a variety of project management and user testing tools.

Website Design & Development

My role as a web designer is to craft visually appealing and user-friendly websites that will effectively communicate your brand’s message. I start by understanding your goals and target audience, then create design concepts and layouts that align with your vision. I focus on creating a cohesive visual style, including choosing color schemes, typography, and imagery, and ensure that the design is responsive and works well across different devices and screen sizes. When implementing designs, I prioritize accessibility and ensure key design elements are properly developed to properly execute approved designs. Throughout the process, I keep the user experience in mind to create a site that is both engaging and easy to navigate, while also staying true to your brand identity.

On previous web design projects, I have worked with Figma, WordPress, Github, and a variety of project management tools.

Logo Design

As a logo designer, my job is to create distinctive and memorable logos that capture the essence of your brand. I begin by understanding your business or organization, target audience, and brand values, which helps me design a logo that will visually represent your identity. I work on developing various concepts and refining them based on feedback to ensure the final design is both unique and versatile. My goal is to create a logo that stands out, is easily recognizable, and works well across different mediums (such as web, print, and merchandise) and sizes. I will also provide guidance on how to use the logo effectively in various contexts to maintain brand consistency.

On previous logo design projects, I have worked with Figma, Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe Illustrator.
